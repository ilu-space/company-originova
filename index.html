Our Methodology

Six capabilities.
One cohesive process.

We run a structured, repeatable commercialization process designed for research institutions — from the first portfolio scan to a funded, validated opportunity.

🔬
Step 01

Research Intelligence Scanning

AI-powered analysis of your university's publication and patent portfolio to identify research with the highest commercial potential, scoring for market size, feasibility, and timing.

⚡
Step 02

Rapid Prototyping

Our product and engineering team builds functional prototypes from selected research — translating complex science into tangible demos that investors and customers can experience.

📊
Step 03

Demand Validation

We run structured market experiments — landing pages, buyer interviews, LOI campaigns — to prove (or disprove) real-world demand before any major investment is made.

🗺️
Step 04

Commercialization Roadmap

A clear, actionable go-to-market plan for each validated opportunity: recommended business model, target customers, IP strategy, and funding pathway — spinout, license, or partnership.

🤝
Step 05

Investor & Industry Matchmaking

We connect validated research-products with our network of deep-tech investors, corporate partners, and grant programs to secure the next stage of funding and development.

🏛️
Step 06

TTO Capacity Extension

Designed to augment — not replace — your technology transfer office. We integrate with your existing processes, IP policies, and researcher relationships as a seamless extension of your team.
